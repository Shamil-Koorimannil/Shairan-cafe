The Beginning

Irani chai itself isn’t originally from Kerala. It comes from Persian/Irani cafés in India established by Iranian immigrants in the 19th century, especially in cities like Hyderabad and Mumbai. These cafés became known for their rich, slow-brewed tea and distinctive café culture.

Our Mission

To offer an authentic and premium Irani café experience by combining traditional recipes, Persian-inspired décor, and modern operational excellence.

BE BOLD, IRANI BREWED

Our Vision

To establish Shairan Café as an internationally recognized Irani tea brand, spreading the heritage of Persian chai culture across the world.
